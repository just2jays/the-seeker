Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-11

The Seeker - Cute Virtual Pet & Desktop Buddy is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of our Chrome extension.

1. What Data We Collect

The Seeker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or user activity. All information related to your use of the extension stays entirely local to your device.

2. How Data Is Stored

We use the Chrome Extension storage.local API to save your configuration preferences. This includes:

  • Your active character choice (e.g., Cat Soldier, Sir Nerdington, The Robot, Puppy)
  • Movement mode selection (Wander randomly vs. Follow mouse)
  • Dialogue speech bubble preferences (Enabled vs. Disabled)
  • Companion size scale and walking speed settings
  • Companion sleep/wake state

This data is stored locally in your browser profile and is never sent to any external server or third-party service.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

This extension does not use any cookies, analytic scripts (such as Google Analytics), tracking telemetry, or third-party web services. It runs 100% offline.

4. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any of your data, we do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at developer@worldisending.com.
